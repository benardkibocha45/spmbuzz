Who We Are

Redefining Entertainment and Digital Storytelling in Kenya

SPM Buzz is a Kenyan digital media company powered by Stage Presence Media (SPM Africa). Founded by comedian and entrepreneur Eddie Butita, we tell stories that resonate with the culture, passions, and aspirations of our audience.

  • Delivering fresh entertainment, lifestyle, and trending stories daily.
  • Creating platforms that amplify Kenyan voices and talent.
  • Bridging entertainment and impact by producing compelling digital content across platforms.
